Frequently asked questions

What are wooden blinds? + Wooden blinds are window coverings made from real wood or composite materials, featuring adjustable slats for light control. They add natural warmth and elegance, standing out for their durability and eco-friendliness.
Are they suitable for all rooms? + Yes, they enhance any room’s aesthetics. However, for high-humidity areas like bathrooms, alternatives like faux wood blinds are better suited.
How do I clean them? + Dust regularly with a soft cloth. For deep cleaning, use a damp cloth with mild soap and dry immediately. Avoid harsh chemicals.
