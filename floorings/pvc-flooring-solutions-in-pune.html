Frequently asked questions

What is PVC flooring, and what are its benefits? + PVC flooring is a type of resilient flooring made from polyvinyl chloride. It is known for its exceptional durability, aesthetic appeal, waterproof properties, cost-effectiveness, comfort, and noise reduction. It is an ideal choice for both residential and commercial spaces due to its ability to withstand heavy foot traffic and various environmental conditions.
How does PVC flooring compare to other flooring options? + PVC flooring is more durable and resistant to wear and tear than many traditional flooring materials. It is also waterproof, making it suitable for moisture-prone areas like kitchens and bathrooms. Additionally, it tends to be more affordable than other flooring options such as hardwood or tile, without compromising on style and quality.
Is PVC flooring easy to maintain? + Yes, PVC flooring is very easy to maintain. Cleaning typically requires just a simple wipe with a damp cloth or mop to remove dirt and spills. This low-maintenance characteristic makes it a practical choice for busy households and commercial spaces.
Can PVC flooring be installed in wet areas? + Absolutely! One of the significant advantages of PVC flooring is its waterproof nature, making it an excellent choice for wet areas such as kitchens, bathrooms, and laundry rooms. It can handle moisture without swelling or warping.
