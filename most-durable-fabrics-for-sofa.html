Factors to Consider When Choosing Durable Sofa Fabric

  1. Abrasion Resistance

    The durability of a fabric is often measured by its rub count, which indicates how well it withstands friction. Fabrics with a high rub count, such as those offered by Crescent Furnishings, are ideal for high-traffic areas.

  2. Stain Resistance

    For environments prone to spills, opt for fabrics treated with stain-resistant finishes or those made from naturally stain-resistant materials like synthetic blends.

  3. Fade Resistance

    If your sofa will be exposed to sunlight, choose fabrics designed to resist fading, such as solution-dyed acrylic or polyester.

  4. Texture and Weave

    Tight weaves are generally more durable than loose weaves, as they resist snags and wear better.

  5. Cleaning Requirements

    Fabrics that are easy to clean, such as those with removable and washable covers, are ideal for businesses and homes with children or pets.

Care Tips for Durable Sofa Fabrics


  • Regular Cleaning: Vacuum or wipe down fabrics weekly to remove dust and dirt.

  • Use Protective Covers: In high-traffic areas, covers can protect against spills and stains.

  • Rotate Cushions: Regularly rotate cushions to distribute wear evenly.

  • Address Spills Immediately: Blot (don’t rub) spills to prevent stains from setting.

  • Professional Cleaning: Schedule periodic professional cleaning for materials like wool or leather.
