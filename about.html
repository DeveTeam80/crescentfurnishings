EXPLORING CRESCENT

Crescent Furnishings India LLP has been a prominent presence in Pune’s furnishing landscape for over four decades. Founded in 2016 by Mr. Oveez Khan and Mr. Sohail Khan, our establishment draws from the esteemed legacy of the Khan family, renowned pioneers in the industry since 1985. Inspired by their father, Mr. Afzal Khan’s visionary leadership, Oveez and Sohail embarked on a mission to redefine luxury and establish a global presence.

OUR MISSION

To offer high-quality furnishing fabrics, exceptional service, and an inspiring shopping experience, blending style, comfort, and functionality.

OUR VISION

To be the premier destination for exquisite furnishing fabrics, where luxury meets affordability and dreams find expression.

OUR STORE

Located in Crescent Plaza, our flagship store spans four floors and showcases luxury fabrics. Our dedicated team ensures every visit is unforgettable.

OUR PROMISE

At Crescent Furnishings, we craft spaces with quality and innovation. We create homes that reflect your style. Welcome to luxury and affordability.
