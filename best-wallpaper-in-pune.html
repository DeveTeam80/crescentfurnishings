Wallpapers play a significant role in defining the ambiance of a room. The right wallpaper can make a small room appear spacious, add depth to a plain wall, or create a cozy, inviting atmosphere. With advancements in wallpaper materials and printing technology, homeowners and designers can choose from a wide range of textures, patterns, and finishes that suit their vision. Standard Size Wallpapers: Convenience and Elegance

Standard-size wallpapers are an excellent option for those looking for quick and hassle-free wall transformations. These wallpapers come in pre-defined dimensions that fit most wall sizes, making installation straightforward and efficient.


Most standard wallpapers are available in rolls of 53 cm to 106 cm in width and 10 m to 15 m in length, ensuring they cover substantial wall space while maintaining seamless design flow. The advantage of choosing standard-size wallpapers from Crescent Furnishings LLP is the ease of availability, affordability, and diverse design selection. Whether you want floral, geometric, textured, or abstract designs, our collection offers a plethora of choices to complement your interiors.

Material Choices for Wallpapers

Wallpapers come in a variety of materials, each offering distinct advantages in terms of aesthetics, durability, and maintenance. At Crescent Furnishings LLP, we provide the following wallpaper materials:



  • Vinyl Wallpapers:

    Durable, water-resistant, and easy to clean, making them ideal for kitchens and bathrooms.

  • Non-Woven Wallpapers:

    Breathable, tear-resistant, and easy to install and remove.

  • Textured Wallpapers:

    Adds depth and dimension to walls with tactile finishes.

  • Fabric Wallpapers:

    Luxurious and elegant, perfect for upscale interiors.

  • Peel-and-Stick Wallpapers:

    Hassle-free and temporary, great for renters or those who frequently change decor.

    • Choosing the right material depends on the location, expected wear and tear, and the overall aesthetic you wish to achieve.



Benefits of Using Wallpapers for Interior Wall Decor

Wallpapers offer several advantages over traditional wall paints and decorative elements:



  • Aesthetic Appeal:

    Instantly elevates the visual appeal of a space with rich colors and intricate designs.

  • Durability:

    High-quality wallpapers can last up to 10-15 years, making them a cost-effective investment.

  • Easy Maintenance:

    Many wallpapers are washable and resistant to stains, ensuring long-term beauty.

  • Quick Installation:

    Unlike painting, which requires multiple coats and drying time, wallpapers can be applied in just a few hours.

  • Customization:

    Personalize your space with unique designs that reflect your personality and style.

How to Select the Perfect Wallpaper for Your Space

  1. Room Type:

    Instantly elevates the visual appeal of a space with rich colors and intricate designs.

  2. Color Scheme:

    Lighter shades make a room appear more spacious, while darker tones create a cozy and dramatic effect.

  3. Patterns & Textures:

    Geometric patterns add a modern touch, floral designs bring elegance, and textured wallpapers provide depth.

  4. Lighting Conditions:

    Glossy and metallic wallpapers reflect light, making rooms appear brighter, while matte finishes work well in well-lit spaces.

  5. Wall Surface:

    Ensure walls are smooth and clean for a flawless wallpaper application.
