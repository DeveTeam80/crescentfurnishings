When to Buy Curtains in Pune – Style , Function And Seasonal Comforts

  1. Home / Blog / Curtain Store In Pune
  • 14th June
  • 2 Comments

Curtains aren’t just décor—they control light, offer privacy, protect interiors from sun damage, and help regulate temperature. In Pune, with its mix of hot summers, damp monsoons, and cool winters, choosing the right curtain at the right time enhances both the look and comfort of your home.

Summer (February to May): Let Light In, Keep Heat Out

Pune’s pre-monsoon months are hot and dry. During this time, opt for light-colored cotton or linen curtains that reflect heat while letting air flow. Sheer curtains are also great for brightening interiors without harsh glare. Neutral shades like white, beige, or pastel blue create a cool, breezy vibe.
Best styles: Eyelet cotton, sheer panels, blackout lining for west-facing windows
Recommended spaces: Living rooms, study areas, balconies


Monsoon (June to August): Fight Moisture & Mold

Humidity and occasional mold growth are common during Pune’s rainy season. Choose mildew-resistant polyester blends or treated fabrics that dry easily and resist odor. Avoid heavy drapes that retain moisture.
Best styles: Synthetic blends, shorter panels, easily washable materials
Recommended spaces: Bedrooms, kitchens, entryways near balconies


Winter (October to January): Cozy & Insulating

As Pune cools down, especially in December and January, thicker curtains like velvet, suede, or layered drapes help retain warmth and provide insulation. They also block chilly drafts, especially in homes with large windows or open balconies.
Best styles: Layered curtains, double rods with sheers + blackout
Recommended spaces: Bedrooms, media rooms, main halls


Best Times to Buy Curtains in Pune

Crescent Furnishing offers year-round style, but these periods are great for deals:
Gudi Padwa & Diwali: Major home décor sales and combos
New Year & Republic Day: End-of-season discounts
Mid-year refresh (June–July): Great time for summer-to-monsoon transition curtains

Choosing the right curtains for your home in Pune isn’t just about color or texture—it’s about understanding how each season influences your comfort and interior aesthetics. From airy cottons that keep your space cool in the summer to rich, insulating drapes for Pune’s chilly winter mornings, your curtain choices should evolve with the weather. The right fabric and style can significantly reduce glare, protect furniture from sun damage, and even help regulate indoor temperature, making your home more energy-efficient and comfortable year-round.
