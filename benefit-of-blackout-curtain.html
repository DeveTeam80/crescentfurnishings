What Are Blackout Curtains?
Blackout curtains are specially designed window coverings made with tightly woven fabric that blocks out sunlight and reduces noise. Unlike regular curtains, they are equipped with a lining or multiple layers of fabric to ensure complete opacity. Blackout curtains are available in various styles, colors, and textures, allowing them to blend seamlessly with any home décor.
