The Digital Printing Process: A Revolution in Textile


The process of digital printing on fabric begins with a high-resolution design file. This file is fed into a digital printer equipped with advanced inkjet technology. The printer applies eco-friendly inks directly onto the fabric, ensuring precision and vibrancy. The fabric is then subjected to a fixation process, locking the colors and patterns in place. This method eliminates the need for stencils, plates, or other tools used in traditional printing, making it a highly efficient and sustainable choice.
